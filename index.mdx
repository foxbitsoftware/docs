## Crunchforms
You can connect forms or other events to Crunchforms in minutes and start receiving submissions instantly, with zero server-side code. 

### No Backend Required
Crunchforms is great for quickly spinning up landing pages or static websites without the burden of needing to host or maintaining server code - even for form submissions. This will help you dramatically reduce the cost of maintenance and overhead for you and your clients.
